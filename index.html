Jewellery and Creative Arts

Bubble Ring

** Long description **. This unique ring is crafted from delicate, shimmering bubbles, each one encased in a smooth, translucent setting that captures the light with every movement. The bubbles appear to float around the band, giving the piece an ethereal and playful quality. The design mimics the soft, fleeting nature of bubbles, with a subtle iridescence that shifts in colour as the ring is worn. The band itself is lightweight yet sturdy, ensuring comfort while maintaining the illusion of fragility.
